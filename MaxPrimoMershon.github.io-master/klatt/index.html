About Klatt

Welcome to Klatt Temporary Service! We are a certified Women-Owned Business Enterprise (WBE) conveniently located in the heart of downtown Chicago. Our company was founded in 1975 by Jim Klatt. Judy Davis joined the Klatt team in 1980 and purchased Klatt Employment Services, Inc. in November 2002. Since our inception, Klatt has built a reputation of outstanding service among our clients by providing superior temporary employees throughout the city.

Whether you are a Human Resources expert looking to fill a position or an administrative professional seeking a new challenge, Klatt Temporary Service has the resources to assist you. Let us tell you how we can recruit a new employee or help job seekers with a new opportunity. We at Klatt are here to match the ideal candidate with the perfect position
