  • Attitude- We realize that going to new companies, meeting different people, and experiencing new jobs can be difficult. That is why a good attitude can create such a positive relationship between you, Klatt Temporary Service, and our clients.
  • Dependability- Making good first impressions with new supervisors can make your entire transition run more smoothly. Temporary employees who are continuously punctual are asked back again and again.
  • Professionalism- Dressing for success, limiting personal calls and displaying a professional, helpful demeanor makes a long lasting impression on anyone you may meet on a job.
  • Productivity- Employees who are efficient and productive are often asked back on new assignments with the same company. You can almost always avoid mistakes by asking questions first. Remember - most clients view questions as interest and concern about the work you are doing and the impression you are making on them.

Klatt pays weekly on Fridays, the same week you work. If you work for us this week, you will be paid this Friday. Please call in your hours anytime Thursday. Estimate the week's total hours worked including Friday's hours if you are scheduled to work on Friday.

Anyone who has missed Friday's payroll deadline or is working on the weekend must call in hours by 10:00 a.m. the following Monday.

Friday payroll checks will be ready at noon on Friday and Monday paychecks will be ready at noon on Monday.

In order to pick up a check, you are responsible for following these guidelines:

  1. You must bring in the first two copies of your timesheet that has been signed by your supervisor.
  2. Timesheets are similar to checks. We will not accept timesheets with any cross-outs or white-outs. If you need to make any adjustments, please fill out another timesheet before it is signed by your supervisor.

Checks may be picked up between 12:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m. on Friday. If you would like to pick up your checks after 5:00 p.m., please let us know on Friday. We can make arrangements with the building security.

If you would like us to mail your check, we must receive your timesheet first. Please fax us your timesheet on Friday, and send us the original in the mail.
